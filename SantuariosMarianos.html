Journey Details

Fatima, Portugal (3 Nights)

Join us for a unique faith experience at the Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima, one of the world's most important Marian pilgrimage sites. We will participate in the moving torchlight procession and pray the Holy Rosary in more than five languages alongside pilgrims from around the world.

We will visit the Chapel of Apparitions, the Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary, and the impressive Basilica of the Holy Trinity. We will also explore the small village of Aljustrel, where the houses of the three little shepherds to whom the Virgin Mary appeared are located.

Recognized as one of the world's most important Marian sanctuaries, Fatima is a place of deep devotion and faith. Here, the Virgin Mary appeared to three shepherd children in 1917, leaving a message of conversion, prayer, and hope that continues to touch hearts today.

Pilgrims can participate in praying the rosary, candlelight processions, and Eucharistic adoration, as well as visit the Chapel of Apparitions, the Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary, and the modern Basilica of the Holy Trinity.

Lourdes, France (4 Nights)

Experience a profound faith journey in Lourdes, the sanctuary where the Virgin Mary appeared to Saint Bernadette. We will visit the Grotto of Apparitions and participate in the torchlight procession and Holy Rosary prayer in more than 5 languages, together with pilgrims from around the world.

We will have the opportunity to participate in the water ritual, a deeply spiritual moment. This simple act connects us with the Virgin Mary's message to Saint Bernadette and with the miracles that, over time, have strengthened the faith of millions of people.

We will retrace the steps of Saint Bernadette and visit the Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary, the Basilica of the Immaculate Conception, and the modern underground Basilica of Saint Pius X. We will also participate in the Eucharistic procession and moments of adoration.

One of the world's most visited pilgrimage destinations, famous for the apparitions of the Virgin Mary to Saint Bernadette Soubirous in 1858. This sanctuary is a symbol of faith, healing, and hope for millions of pilgrims who come each year.

The Grotto of Massabielle, where the apparitions occurred, is the spiritual heart of the sanctuary, along with the basilicas that surround it. Many pilgrims come to the pools to immerse themselves in the waters considered miraculous or simply to drink from the springs, following the message of penance and conversion that the Virgin left in this sacred place.

Athens, Greece (1 Night)

Let yourself be amazed by the majesty of the Acropolis and its imposing temples that still stand as witnesses of time. In the midst of this classical legacy, we will pause to contemplate one of the most powerful moments of early Christianity: Saint Paul's discourse at the Areopagus, where he boldly announced the "unknown God" to the wise men of his time (Acts 17:22-31).

Walking through these places means delving into the history of the Gospel, where faith confronted reason, and the Word of God began to illuminate the Greek world.

Athens occupies a special place in the history of Christianity. In this city, Saint Paul preached at the Areopagus. Pilgrims can climb the Acropolis and walk the same paths the apostle traveled, contemplating the contrast between Christian faith and Greek wisdom of the time.

It is an opportunity to renew the call to courageous witness, like that of Saint Paul, and reflect on the encounter between faith and reason in this historic place.

Greek Islands Cruise (7 Nights)

Embark on a magnificent 7-night cruise through the Greek Islands, following the maritime routes that Saint Paul traveled during his missionary journeys. This unique experience combines the beauty of the Aegean Sea with profound spiritual reflection as we visit the islands and coastal cities where early Christianity took root.

We will visit ancient Corinth, where Saint Paul founded one of the first Christian communities and wrote his famous epistles. In this ancient port city, where culture and commerce intertwined, we will immerse ourselves in the origins of the Church and the powerful message of love, unity, and living in the Spirit that the apostle preached.

Explore the stunning Greek islands while reflecting on Saint Paul's courage and dedication as he spread the Gospel throughout the Mediterranean world. Each port of call offers both natural beauty and spiritual significance.

The Greek Islands and coastal cities played a crucial role in early Christianity. Saint Paul's missionary journeys through these waters established some of the first Christian communities outside of Palestine, making this cruise a journey through the very foundations of our faith.

Corinth, one of the most influential cities of ancient Greece, occupies a central place in the history of early Christianity. It was there that Saint Paul founded one of the first Christian communities and wrote two of his best-known letters, inviting them to live in love, unity, and faithfulness to the Gospel.
