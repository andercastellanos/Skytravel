Journey Details

Fatima

Visit the Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima, participate in the candlelight procession, and enjoy personal prayer time at this sacred site where the Virgin Mary appeared to three shepherd children.

Lourdes

Experience the sacred Grotto of Apparitions, join the evening Marian procession, and have the opportunity to visit the healing baths where countless miracles have been reported.

Medjugorje

Visit Apparition Hill (Podbrdo), climb Cross Mountain (Križevac), and spend time in spiritual reflection at this modern site of Marian devotion.
