Detailed Information About Medjugorje

St. James Church

The center of worship in Medjugorje, St. James Church hosts daily Masses in multiple languages and evening programs that draw thousands of pilgrims. The church's outdoor altar area can accommodate over 5,000 people.

Experience the powerful community prayer, healing services, and the profound peace that permeates this sacred space.

Apparition Hill (Podbrdo)

This is where the Virgin Mary first appeared to six young visionaries in 1981. Today, pilgrims climb the rocky path while praying the Rosary, stopping at bronze reliefs depicting the mysteries.

The Blue Cross at the base of the hill is a special place of prayer and peace, where many visionaries still receive apparitions.

Cross Mountain (Krizevac)

The massive concrete cross atop Mount Krizevac was built in 1933 to commemorate the 1900th anniversary of Jesus's passion and death. The mountain's steep path includes bronze reliefs of the Stations of the Cross.

Many pilgrims climb the mountain barefoot as an act of penance, especially on Fridays.

Evening Program

The evening prayer program is the heartbeat of Medjugorje. Beginning with the Rosary, followed by Holy Mass, and often including Eucharistic Adoration or Veneration of the Cross, these evenings are profound spiritual experiences.

International simultaneous translation is available for pilgrims from around the world.
