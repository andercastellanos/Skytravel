Welcome to Our Blog

This space is born from prayer, from silence, and from a journey traveled.

More than seventeen years ago, I had a personal encounter with the Lord that transformed my life. Since then, the Word of God, the Catechism of the Church, constant formation, persevering prayer, and spiritual accompaniment have been part of my daily life. Not as theory, but as lived experience.

Throughout these years, I have accompanied people, parents, families, married couples, and wounded hearts. I have witnessed deep struggles, difficult relationships, disordered attachments, and decisions made from fear or loneliness. But I have also seen God's faithfulness, His infinite patience, and the fruits that are born when one walks with perseverance and faith.

This blog does not pretend to teach from a pedestal or impose answers. It is born from the desire to share spiritual reflections that help discern, choose better, and look at one's own story with more truth and hope. Especially those decisions that mark one's entire life, such as love, marriage, and vocation.

I write from experience, from accompaniment, and from a life that has been sustained by prayer. I write because many people have asked me to, because my son insistently encouraged me to do so, and because I believe there are truths that need to be said with love, clarity, and mercy.

If these reflections help you to pray, to pause, to open your eyes, or to trust more in God's timing, then this space will have fulfilled its mission. When you are ready to plan, explore our travel experiences or reach us through contact.
