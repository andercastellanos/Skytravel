Medjugorje 2017

Encountering faith at the site of apparitions

← Back to All Experiences

Our 2017 pilgrimage to Medjugorje offered a profound spiritual experience in the peaceful hills of Bosnia and Herzegovina. Pilgrims climbed Apparition Hill (Podbrdo) where the Virgin Mary first appeared to six young visionaries in 1981, and prayed the Stations of the Cross while ascending Cross Mountain (Križevac). The group participated in daily Mass at St. James Church and joined the evening program with thousands of pilgrims from around the world. Many reported experiencing a special sense of peace, spiritual renewal, and deepened prayer life during this transformative journey to a place where heaven touches earth.
