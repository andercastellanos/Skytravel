Medjugorje 2024

Spiritual renewal in Bosnia and Herzegovina

← Back to All Experiences

In 2024, we embarked on a transformative pilgrimage to Medjugorje, experiencing the profound peace and spiritual renewal that this sacred site offers. Our journey included prayer on Apparition Hill, meditation on Cross Mountain, and participation in the powerful evening programs at St. James Church.
