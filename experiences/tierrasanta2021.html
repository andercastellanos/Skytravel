Holy Land Pilgrimage 2021

Walking in the footsteps of Jesus

← Back to All Experiences

In 2021, our pilgrimage to the Holy Land took us on a profound spiritual journey through the sites where Jesus lived, ministered, died, and rose again. Despite the challenges of the post-pandemic world, our group experienced the deep spiritual connection and transformation that comes from walking the paths of Biblical history. From Jerusalem to Bethlehem, from the Sea of Galilee to Nazareth, this journey brought the Scriptures to life for all participants.
