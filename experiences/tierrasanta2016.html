Holy Land Pilgrimage 2016

Following the footsteps of Jesus in the Sacred Land

← Back to All Experiences

Our 2016 pilgrimage to the Holy Land offered participants a transformative spiritual journey through the sacred sites of Christianity. Pilgrims followed in the footsteps of Jesus from Bethlehem to Jerusalem, experiencing the profound connection to places mentioned in Scripture. The group celebrated Mass at the Church of the Holy Sepulchre, prayed at the Western Wall, walked the Via Dolorosa, and visited the Sea of Galilee. Time spent in Nazareth, Bethlehem, and other key biblical locations brought the Gospel stories to life in a tangible way. For many pilgrims, this journey deepened their faith and provided a renewed understanding of the historical and geographical context of Christianity's origins.

Ready to experience your own spiritual transformation in the Holy Land? Join our next Holy Land pilgrimage and walk where Jesus walked, creating memories that will last a lifetime.
