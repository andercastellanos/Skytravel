Tierra Santa 2019

A transformative journey through Israel

← Back to All Experiences

Our 2019 pilgrimage to the Holy Land was a transformative journey through the sacred sites of Christianity. We followed the footsteps of Jesus from Bethlehem to Jerusalem, and from the shores of the Sea of Galilee to the heights of Mount Tabor. Pilgrims participated in daily Mass at sacred locations, prayed in the Church of the Holy Sepulchre, touched the waters of the Jordan River, and experienced the profound spiritual significance of the places where our faith began. This voyage of faith created lasting memories and spiritual growth for all participants.
