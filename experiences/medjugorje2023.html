Medjugorje 2023

A journey of faith and prayer

← Back to All Experiences

In 2023, our pilgrimage to Medjugorje offered a profound opportunity for spiritual growth and renewal. Our group experienced the unique peace of this sacred place, climbing Apparition Hill where the Virgin Mary first appeared, praying the Way of the Cross on Cross Mountain, and participating in the powerful evening programs at St. James Church. Through prayer, reconciliation, and community, many pilgrims experienced life-changing moments of grace and healing.
