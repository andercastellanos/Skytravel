Medjugorje 2022

Experiencing the presence of Our Lady

← Back to All Experiences

In 2022, our pilgrimage to Medjugorje was a deeply moving spiritual experience for all participants. Our group climbed Apparition Hill where the Virgin Mary first appeared to the visionaries, prayed the Stations of the Cross on Cross Mountain, and participated in the powerful evening programs at St. James Church. Pilgrims had time for personal reflection, confession, and community worship in this sacred place where many report experiencing a special peace and spiritual renewal. As one pilgrim shared, "The presence of Mary's love was palpable throughout our journey, touching hearts and transforming lives."
