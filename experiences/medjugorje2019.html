Medjugorje 2019

Prayer and reflection in a place of apparitions

← Back to All Experiences

In 2019, our pilgrimage to Medjugorje offered an extraordinary spiritual experience in the heart of Bosnia and Herzegovina. Pilgrims climbed Apparition Hill where the Virgin Mary first appeared to the visionaries, prayed the Stations of the Cross on Cross Mountain, and participated in the powerful evening programs at St. James Church. The peaceful atmosphere, daily prayer services, and community with other pilgrims from around the world created an environment of profound spiritual renewal. Many pilgrims reported feeling a special sense of peace and the presence of Our Lady during this transformative journey.
