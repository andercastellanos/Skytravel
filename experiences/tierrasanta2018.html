Tierra Santa 2018

Exploring the biblical landmarks of Israel

← Back to All Experiences

In 2018, our pilgrimage to the Holy Land offered pilgrims an unforgettable journey through biblical history. The group visited sacred sites including the Church of the Nativity in Bethlehem, the Basilica of the Annunciation in Nazareth, and the Garden of Gethsemane. Walking the Via Dolorosa and celebrating Mass at the Church of the Holy Sepulchre were powerful spiritual experiences. Pilgrims also enjoyed a boat ride on the Sea of Galilee, visited Capernaum where Jesus based his ministry, and renewed their baptismal promises at the Jordan River. Throughout the journey, Scripture came alive as travelers experienced the landscapes and holy sites where Jesus and the early Christians lived and ministered.
