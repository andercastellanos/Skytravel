Tierra Santa 2022

Walking in the footsteps of Jesus

← Back to All Experiences

In 2022, our pilgrimage to the Holy Land offered a profound spiritual journey through the places where Jesus lived and ministered. Our group traveled through Bethlehem, Jerusalem, Nazareth, the Sea of Galilee, and many other biblical sites. From praying at the birthplace of Christ to walking the Via Dolorosa and celebrating Mass at the Holy Sepulchre, pilgrims experienced the Gospels coming alive in these sacred locations. Many described the journey as transformative, deepening their faith and understanding of Scripture through this tangible encounter with the roots of Christianity.
