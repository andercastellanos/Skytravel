Holy Land Pilgrimage 2017

A transformative spiritual journey to the Sacred Land

← Back to All Experiences

Our 2017 pilgrimage to the Holy Land was a deeply moving spiritual journey through the land where Jesus lived, ministered, died, and rose again. Pilgrims visited Bethlehem's Church of the Nativity, prayed in Jerusalem's Church of the Holy Sepulchre, and walked the Via Dolorosa. They shared moments of reflection on the Mount of Olives and the Garden of Gethsemane. The group also sailed on the Sea of Galilee, visited the Mount of Beatitudes, and experienced Capernaum where Jesus based much of his ministry. Many pilgrims described this journey as life-changing, bringing the Scriptures to life in profound ways as they connected physically with the sacred places of their faith.

Experience your own spiritual awakening in the Holy Land. Join our upcoming Holy Land pilgrimage and discover the transformative power of walking where Jesus walked.
